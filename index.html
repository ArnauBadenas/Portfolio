ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Arnau, a web developer with a lot of passion for bringing projects to live.

As a developer, I'm proficient in a variety of programming languages, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python. I'm also skilled in using popular frameworks like React, Vue, and Angular, and I have experience working with databases like MySQL and MongoDB.
